About the Server

Welcome to Washington State Roleplay, we are the largest ER:LC Washington State server. We offer immersive Roleplay as well as One of the best Staff Teams. So, what are ou waiting for? Join us now!

Server Rules

Respect

Disrespecting other users will result in mutes or kicks.

Drama

Starting or participating in drama will result in kicks or bans.

Profanity

Profanity is allowed, but slurs will result in bans.

Mass Pinging

Mass pinging staff will result in mutes or kicks.

Discord TOS

Violating the Discord TOS will result in bans.

NSFW

Sharing NSFW content will result in bans.

Advertising

Advertising without owner consent will result in kicks or bans.

Game Rules

RDM

RDM (Random Death Match) will result in Warnings + Kicks.

VDM

VDM (Vehicle Death Match) will result in Warnings + Kicks.

FRP

FRP (Fail Roleplay) will result in Warnings + Kicks.

NITRP

NITRP (No Intent To Roleplay) will result in a Ban.

NLR

NLR (New Life Rule) will result in Warnings + Kicks.

RTAP & LTAP

RTAP (Respawning To Avoid Punishment) & LTAP (Leaving To Avoid Punishment) will result in Bans.

Banned RP

Banned RPs (such as NSFW, Suicide, Terrorist, etc) will result in Bans.

Staff Disrespect

Staff Disrespect will result in Kicks + Bans.

Member Disrespect

Member Disrespect will result in Warnings + Kicks.

Roblox TOS

Breaking Roblox TOS will result in an Immediate Ban.

SRDM

SRDM (Staff RDM) will result in Kicks + Bans.

SVDM

SVDM (Staff VDM) will result in Kicks + Bans.

Interrupting STS

Interrupting STS will result in Kicks + Bans.

Staff Evasion

Staff Evasion will result in Kicks + Bans.

Lying to Staff

Lying to Staff will result in Warnings, Kicks, and Bans.

Failure to Adhere to Staff.

Failure to Adhere to Staff will result in Kicks + Bans.
